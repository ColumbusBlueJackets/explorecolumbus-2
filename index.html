Meet Aaron Johnson

Aaron Johnson rejoined the Columbus Blue Jackets as the clubâ€™s team relations specialist, prior to the 2021-22 season. His role includes managing operational logistics, specifically lifestyle services and hospitality events, for players and hockey operations personnel with a goal of ensuring that the needs of players and their families are met and that their time in Columbus is a positive and engaging experience.

Johnson is a native of Port Hawkesbury, Nova Scotia and was selected by Columbus in the third round of the 2001 NHL Draft. A defenseman, he appeared in 291 career NHL games with the Blue Jackets, New York Islanders, Chicago Blackhawks, Calgary Flames, Edmonton Oilers, and Boston Bruins. He also played professionally in Germany and England during a 17-year playing career from 2003-20. Aaron received his MBA from The University of Sheffield Management School in the UK. He and his wife, Courtney, are the parents of son, Knox. The family resides in New Albany.
